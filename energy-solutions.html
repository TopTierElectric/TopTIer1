Efficiency Starts With the Electrical Plan

Energy solutions are about targeted upgrades that match how you use the space. We focus on practical improvements—better lighting, smarter controls, and right-sized circuits—without promising unrealistic savings.

Practical Upgrades We Install

  • LED fixture replacements and retrofits for consistent, efficient lighting.
  • Dimmer switches, occupancy sensors, and timers for control and comfort.
  • Circuit updates that reduce overloaded circuits and nuisance trips.
  • Dedicated circuits for high-demand equipment to reduce strain.

Our Process

  1. Assess: Review current lighting, controls, and circuit usage.
  2. Plan: Outline upgrade options and prioritize the best return for your space.
  3. Install: Complete the electrical work with clean wiring and labeled circuits.
  4. Confirm: Test operation and explain new controls or settings.

What’s Included

  • Electrical evaluation focused on efficiency opportunities.
  • Installation of lighting, controls, and circuit adjustments.
  • Clear guidance on how to use new switches and settings.

What’s Not Included (Unless Scoped)

  • Whole-home rewires or non-electrical building upgrades.
  • Appliance replacement or non-electrical efficiency services.
  • Guaranteed savings percentages or performance claims.

Energy Solutions FAQs

Do you provide energy audits?

We provide practical assessments focused on electrical upgrades and load planning.

Can you replace old lighting with LEDs?

Yes. We install LED fixtures and retrofits where appropriate.

Do you install smart controls or timers?

Yes. We can add occupancy sensors, timers, and smart switches when compatible.

Will efficiency upgrades reduce my bill?

Results depend on usage and existing equipment. We focus on practical improvements without guaranteed savings.

Can you help plan circuits for new equipment?

Yes. We can right-size circuits and plan for new loads safely.

Do you upgrade panels as part of energy work?

If capacity is limited, we can scope a panel upgrade alongside efficiency changes.

Can you improve lighting comfort?

Yes. We can adjust fixture placement, color temperature, and controls for better comfort.

Do you handle small business upgrades?

We support light commercial projects; call to confirm scope.

Is rewiring included?

Full rewiring is outside typical energy scope unless specifically requested.
