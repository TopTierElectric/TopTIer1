Lighting & Fixtures

Good lighting enhances comfort, productivity and ambiance. Whether you want to brighten your home’s interior, illuminate your yard or upgrade your business’s lighting for energy savings, Top Tier Electrical delivers customised solutions.


Indoor Lighting

We design and install recessed lighting, pendant lights, chandeliers, under‑cabinet lighting and more. Our electricians ensure your lighting not only looks great but also meets electrical code and efficiency standards.


Outdoor & Landscape Lighting

Enhance curb appeal, security and safety with strategically placed outdoor lighting. We provide landscape lighting, pathway lights, deck and patio lighting, and motion‑activated security lighting.


Retrofits & Energy Efficiency

Upgrade outdated fixtures to energy‑efficient LED solutions that lower your utility bills and reduce maintenance. We can audit your current lighting and recommend retrofits that qualify for rebates.


Pricing

Lighting projects vary widely in scope. Simple fixture replacements start at $100 per fixture, while full retrofits or landscape installations are quoted after an on‑site consultation. Contact us for a free lighting assessment.
