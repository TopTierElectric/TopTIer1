Energy Consulting

Energy efficiency isn’t just about saving money—it’s about sustainability and comfort. Our energy consulting services help you reduce your carbon footprint, lower utility bills and adopt renewable solutions like solar and battery storage.


Services Offered

  • Energy Audits: We assess your electrical consumption and identify opportunities for improvement.
  • Solar & Battery Solutions: Design and installation of solar panels and battery storage systems to reduce reliance on the grid.
  • Smart Panels & Load Management: Install Power Control Systems (PCS) that optimise loads and avoid costly service upgrades.
  • Lighting Retrofits: Switch to LED and intelligent lighting controls to cut energy use.
  • Rebate & Incentive Assistance: Navigate utility rebates and tax incentives to maximise your return on investment.

Consultation Process

Our consultants start with a discussion of your goals and challenges. We conduct an on‑site assessment, analyse your utility bills and present a tailored plan. We prioritise solutions based on payback period, environmental impact and your budget.


Pricing

Initial consultations are free. Comprehensive energy audits begin at $250. Implementation costs vary by project scope. Many customers recoup their investment through utility savings and incentives within just a few years.
