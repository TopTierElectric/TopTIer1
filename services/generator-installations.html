Generator Installations

Power outages can happen at any time. A standby generator keeps your home or business running when the grid fails. Top Tier Electrical designs, supplies and installs backup generators tailored to your power needs.


Why Install a Standby Generator?

  • Maintain comfort and safety during storms and outages.
  • Keep essential systems running (refrigeration, heating, sump pumps, medical devices).
  • Protect your business from costly downtime and data loss.
  • Increase property value and peace of mind.

Our Process

We begin with an assessment of your critical loads and recommend the right generator size (natural gas, propane or diesel). Our team handles permitting, fuel connections and electrical integration. We test the system and show you how to operate it. Annual maintenance plans are available to keep your generator ready for the next outage.


Pricing

Standby generator systems start at $5,000 for small residential units and can range upward depending on capacity and features. Contact us for a custom quote based on your needs.
