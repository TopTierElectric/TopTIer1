Surge Protection

Power surges can damage appliances, electronics and critical systems. Whole‑home surge protection defends your investment by diverting excess voltage away from sensitive devices. Top Tier Electrical installs surge protection tailored to your home or business.


Why Choose Whole‑Home Surge Protection?

  • Guard electronics and appliances against lightning strikes and utility spikes.
  • Prevent costly repairs or replacements after surge damage.
  • Add an extra layer of protection beyond plug‑in power strips.
  • Extend the life of your HVAC system, computers and home entertainment equipment.
