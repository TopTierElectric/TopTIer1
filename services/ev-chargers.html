EV Charger Installation

Electric vehicles are the future of transportation. Whether you need a Level 2 charger for your home garage or multiple charging stations for your business, Top Tier Electrical has you covered. We install chargers from leading manufacturers and ensure they are properly wired, safe and compliant.


Residential EV Chargers

Charge your vehicle conveniently at home. We evaluate your electrical system, recommend the right charger and handle the installation from start to finish. Upgrading your panel might be necessary—our electricians will advise you accordingly.


Commercial EV Charging Solutions

Attract customers, tenants or employees with EV charging stations in your parking lot. We design and install commercial charging infrastructure, including multiple units, load management and signage. Financing and rebate information is available upon request.


Pricing

Home installations start at $800 for a standard Level 2 charger (excluding the cost of the unit). Commercial projects are quoted based on quantity, capacity and infrastructure requirements. Contact us for a detailed proposal.
