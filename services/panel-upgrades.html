Panel Upgrades

Your electrical panel is the heart of your home’s electrical system. If your panel is outdated or overloaded, it may struggle to meet modern power demands and pose safety risks. Our licensed electricians upgrade panels to ensure adequate capacity, compliance with code and peace of mind.


Benefits of Upgrading

  • Increased Capacity: Support high‑demand appliances and modern devices.
  • Improved Safety: Reduce the risk of overloads, short circuits and fire hazards.
  • Future‑Proofing: Prepare for additions like EV chargers, hot tubs or home offices.
  • Higher Property Value: Buyers appreciate an upgraded electrical system.

What to Expect

We begin with an on‑site assessment to evaluate your current electrical system. We’ll discuss your needs and provide a transparent quote. When you’re ready, we schedule the upgrade at a time that works for you, handle permits and inspections and ensure minimal downtime.


Pricing

Panel upgrades start at $1,500 for standard 100‑amp panels. Larger homes or complex setups may require a custom quote. Contact us for a free evaluation and we’ll provide a detailed estimate before any work begins.
