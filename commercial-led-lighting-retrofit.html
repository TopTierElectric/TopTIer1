Why retrofit

  • Lower operating cost from reduced energy usage
  • Fewer lamp/ballast failures and lift rentals
  • Improved safety and productivity

Best candidates

  • High bays / low bays
  • Parking lots and exterior wall packs
  • Shop/task lighting
  • Offices, halls, and restrooms

AEO quick answer

How long does a retrofit take?
Many projects can be phased area-by-area to keep operations running, with scheduling around your hours.

Our retrofit process

  1. Walkthrough + goals: brightness targets and problem zones.
  2. Plan + scope: fixture types, controls, and access planning.
  3. Installation: clean wiring, testing, and cleanup.
  4. Handoff: settings and maintenance guidance.

FAQ

What buildings benefit most from LED retrofits?

Warehouses, shops, offices, and exterior site lighting commonly see strong gains in visibility and maintenance reduction.

Can retrofits be installed without shutting down operations?

Often yes. We can phase work by area and align with operational hours.
