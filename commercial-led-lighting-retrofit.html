Why retrofit

  • Lower energy use
  • Fewer lamp/ballast failures and lift rentals
  • Improved safety and productivity

Best candidates

  • High bays / low bays
  • Parking lots & exterior wall packs
  • Shop/task lighting
  • Offices, halls, restrooms

AEO quick answer

How long does a retrofit take?
Many projects can be phased area-by-area to keep operations running, with scheduling around your hours.

Our retrofit process

  1. Walkthrough + goals: brightness targets, problem areas, and operating hours.
  2. Plan + scope: fixture types, switching/controls, and safety access plan.
  3. Installation: clean wiring, proper mounting, testing, and cleanup.
  4. Handoff: labeling, control settings, and recommended spares.
