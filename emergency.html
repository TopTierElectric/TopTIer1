What Counts as an Electrical Emergency?

Electrical problems can be dangerous. If you encounter any of the following, call our emergency hotline immediately:

  • The smell of burning wires or smoke coming from outlets
  • Electric shocks when touching appliances or switches
  • Frequent breaker trips or blown fuses
  • Power outages affecting only your property
  • Exposed wiring or sparking electrical panels

24/7 Response

Our licensed electricians are on call around the clock to respond to emergencies. We aim to arrive promptly and resolve issues safely so you can get back to normal. Please do not attempt to troubleshoot dangerous electrical problems yourself.
