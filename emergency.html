What Counts as an Electrical Emergency?

Electrical problems can be dangerous. If you encounter any of the following, call for urgent assistance:

  • The smell of burning wires or smoke coming from outlets
  • Electric shocks when touching appliances or switches
  • Frequent breaker trips or blown fuses
  • Power outages affecting only your property
  • Exposed wiring or sparking electrical panels

Call 911 First If

  • There is an active fire or heavy smoke
  • Someone has been injured or shocked and needs medical attention
  • You see downed power lines touching vehicles, water, or structures

Call an Electrician If

  • Breakers trip repeatedly after resetting once
  • Outlets feel hot or emit a burning odor
  • Lights flicker or dim when major appliances start
