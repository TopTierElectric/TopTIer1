FAQs

When should I upgrade my electrical panel?

If your home still uses a fuse box, experiences frequent breaker trips or hasn’t been updated in over 30 years, it may be time to upgrade. Modern breaker panels handle higher electrical loads safely and reduce the risk of fire.

Do you install EV chargers?

Yes! We install Level 2 and DC fast chargers for homes and businesses. We also assist with permitting and inspection to ensure everything is up to code.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Holland, Grand Rapids, Byron Center and the surrounding West Michigan communities. See our contact page for more information.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Our company holds a Master Electrician license and carries comprehensive insurance for your protection and peace of mind.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. We provide free, no‑obligation estimates for all projects. Get in touch via our contact page or call us to schedule an appointment.
