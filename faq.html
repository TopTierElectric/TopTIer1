FAQs

⚡ When should I upgrade my electrical panel?

If your panel is outdated, trips frequently, or lacks space for new circuits, an upgrade may be the safest option. Learn more about panel upgrades.

🔔 How long does a panel upgrade take?

Timing depends on the panel layout and scope, but most upgrades are completed in a single day once the plan is confirmed. Request scope details.

🚗 Do you install EV chargers?

Yes. We install Level 2 EV chargers for homes and small businesses with safe routing and proper circuit sizing. Learn more about EV charger installation.

⏱ How long does an EV charger installation take?

Many installations are completed in one visit after the routing and electrical capacity are confirmed. View our EV charger services.

🌎 What areas do you serve?

We serve West Michigan including Holland, Grand Rapids, Byron Center, Zeeland, Saugatuck, Hudsonville, and Allegan. See our service areas page for more information.

📋 Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We are licensed and insured, and the site footer lists MI License #631210. Contact us for details.

💰 Do you provide scope details before work begins?

Yes. We confirm the repair or installation scope before starting work so you know the plan. Get in touch.

Yes, we offer Saturday appointments from 9am to 1pm. Book your appointment.
