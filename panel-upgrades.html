  • Existing panel condition and amperage size

Permits & Inspection Readiness

We handle permits when required and prepare documentation so your upgrade passes inspection without surprises.

When a Panel Upgrade Makes Sense

  • Breakers trip during normal use, or lights flicker under load
  • You’re out of breaker spaces (or planning EV charging, HVAC upgrades, a hot tub, or an addition)
  • The panel shows corrosion, heat damage, or loose/unsafe conditions
  • You want a modern, clearly labeled layout that’s easy to live with long-term

What’s Included (So the Scope Is Clear)

  • Walkthrough of current loads + your future plans
  • Panel assessment (capacity, condition, circuit layout)
  • Permit/inspection coordination when required
  • Clean panel replacement and circuit organization
  • Clear labeling so circuits make sense later
  • Final verification and walkthrough at handoff

How We Work

  1. Walkthrough — We review what you have and what you’re adding.
  2. Plan — Clear scope, load approach, and sequencing.
  3. Execute — Tidy workmanship, organized routing, professional finish.
  4. Verify — Function check, labeling, and a clean handoff.

Why This Matters

Panel work isn’t just “more power.” Done right, it improves safety, reduces nuisance issues, and supports future upgrades without rework. It also makes the home easier to maintain and increases resale confidence with clean, professional electrical work.

FAQ

Will my power be off?
Usually, yes—there is typically a planned outage window while the panel work is completed.

Do I need a full service upgrade?
Not always. We verify capacity and recommend the simplest code-compliant option that fits your goals.

Can you make room for future projects?
Yes—planning for EV charging, generator readiness, and remodel loads is part of a good upgrade.
