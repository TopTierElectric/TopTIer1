Symptoms We Troubleshoot

  • Dead outlets or switches that suddenly stop working.
  • Breakers that trip repeatedly or won’t reset.
  • Partial power loss in one room or across multiple circuits.
  • Lights that flicker, dim, or buzz.
  • Warm outlets, hot breaker panels, or burning odors.

What to Do Now

If you notice heat, burning smells, or sparking, turn off the affected circuit if it’s safe to do so and contact us right away. Avoid repeated breaker resets until the cause is diagnosed.

Our Diagnostic Process

  1. Listen: We start with the symptoms and any recent changes (new appliances, remodels, storms).
  2. Inspect: We check the panel, devices, and visible wiring for wear or loose connections.
  3. Test: We verify circuits, loads, and devices to isolate the issue.
  4. Explain: We walk through the findings and outline repair options before work begins.

What’s Included

  • On-site troubleshooting and circuit testing.
  • Repair or replacement of damaged outlets, switches, or breakers.
  • Corrections for loose or unsafe connections.

What’s Not Included (Unless Scoped)

  • Large remodel rewiring or whole-home rewires.
  • Cosmetic repairs (drywall, paint, trim).
  • Equipment replacement outside the affected circuit.

Electrical Repair FAQs

What should I do if I smell something burning?

Turn off the affected circuit if it’s safe to do so and call for service. We’ll help you determine the next steps.

Can you troubleshoot intermittent power loss?

Yes. We trace the circuit, check connections, and isolate the failing component.

Do you repair aluminum wiring?

We can evaluate aluminum wiring and recommend safe correction options based on what we find.

Is a tripping breaker always a panel issue?

Not always. It can be caused by a faulty device, wiring issue, or an overloaded circuit.

Can you fix outdoor outlets that stop working?

Yes. We test GFCI protection and repair or replace faulty outlets and covers.

Will you provide repair options before work starts?

Yes. We explain findings and outline the repair scope before proceeding.

Do you handle commercial troubleshooting?

We handle light commercial troubleshooting; call to confirm the scope.

Can you fix flickering lights?

Yes. We check fixtures, switches, neutrals, and panel connections to find the cause.

Do I need to replace an outlet that is warm to the touch?

A warm outlet can signal a loose connection. We recommend shutting off the circuit and scheduling service.

Can you help if multiple rooms are out?

Yes. We investigate shared circuits, breakers, and connection points to restore power safely.
