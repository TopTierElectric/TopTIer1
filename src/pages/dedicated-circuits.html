Dedicated Circuits

Dedicated circuits protect high-demand equipment, reduce nuisance tripping, and support safer operation for modern electrical loads. We design and install circuits sized for the equipment you actually use.

When dedicated circuits are needed

  • Adding EV chargers, workshop tools, or major appliances
  • Recurring breaker trips on shared branch circuits
  • Expanding electrical use in garages, additions, or outbuildings

What Top Tier Electrical provides

  • Circuit sizing and route planning
  • Panel connection and labeling updates
  • Code-aware installation and testing
  • Clear scope and upgrade recommendations if required

FAQ

Can one dedicated circuit support multiple devices?

Generally no for major loads; we recommend dedicated circuits sized for each qualifying device to avoid overload risk.

Do you install dedicated circuits for garages and shops?

Yes. We commonly add dedicated circuits for workshops, detached garages, and equipment-specific applications.

Will you label and document new circuits?

Yes. We provide clear panel labeling and explain what each new circuit supports.
