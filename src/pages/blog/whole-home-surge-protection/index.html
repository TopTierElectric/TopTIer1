Whole-home surge protection: what it does

Surges can come from utility events and large equipment cycling on/off in the home. A panel-mounted surge device helps protect sensitive electronics.

  • It complements-not replaces-point-of-use surge strips.
  • It helps protect appliances, controls, and smart home components.
  • It should be matched to panel configuration and electrical demand.
  • Periodic inspection ensures wiring and grounding remain in good shape.

If your home has frequent storm activity or expensive electronics, this is a practical layer of protection.
