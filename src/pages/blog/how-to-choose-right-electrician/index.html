How to choose the right electrician

The best outcomes come from clear expectations and licensed workmanship-not just the lowest quote.

  • Confirm licensing, insurance, and permit familiarity.
  • Ask for a clear scope: what is included, excluded, and scheduled.
  • Check communication speed and whether recommendations are explained.
  • Look for clean, code-aware installations in project photos or reviews.

A reputable contractor should make the process understandable from estimate to final walkthrough.
