Electrical safety tips for your home

Most electrical hazards build slowly. Frequent breaker trips, warm outlets, and flickering lights are all early signals worth taking seriously.

  • Test GFCI outlets monthly in kitchens, baths, garages, and outdoors.
  • Replace damaged cords and avoid permanent use of extension cords.
  • Never ignore a breaker that repeatedly trips under normal load.
  • Schedule an inspection if lights dim when large appliances start.

If you notice any of these warning signs, a licensed electrician can help isolate the root cause safely.
