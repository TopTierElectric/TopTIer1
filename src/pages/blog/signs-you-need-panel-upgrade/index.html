Signs you may need an electrical panel upgrade

Modern homes ask more from electrical systems than ever before. If your panel is overloaded or aging, you may see warning signs before a failure.

  • Breakers trip when using common appliances.
  • There is no room for new circuits.
  • You still have a fuse box or an obsolete panel model.
  • You're adding EV charging, HVAC upgrades, or workshop equipment.

A panel assessment helps determine whether a targeted circuit update or full service upgrade is the right next step.
