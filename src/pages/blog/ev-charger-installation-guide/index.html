EV charger installation guide for homeowners

A quality EV charger install is about more than mounting hardware. Circuit design, load calculations, and cable routing all matter.

  • Verify panel capacity before choosing charger amperage.
  • Select a charger location that balances convenience and cable reach.
  • Plan for weather exposure and future vehicle changes.
  • Use permitted, code-compliant installation for safety and resale value.

Proper planning avoids rework and helps your charger perform reliably in all seasons.
