Our Process

Diagnose → Explain → Scope → Execute & Verify

Our process is designed to eliminate the two biggest frustrations in electrical work: guessing and unclear scope.

  1. Listen & Diagnose
    We start by understanding symptoms and checking the system for a safe, accurate diagnosis.
  2. Plan the Fix
    You get clear options, scope boundaries, and a plan for routing, materials, and safety.
  3. Complete & Walk Through
    We complete the work, test circuits, and walk you through what changed and how to use it.

Emergency Guidance

If you believe you have an electrical emergency:

Do this right now:

  • Leave the area if you smell burning
  • Turn off the main breaker if safe to do so
  • Keep everyone away from downed power lines
  • Call: (616) 334‑7159

Do NOT do this:

  • Do not use water on electrical fires
  • Do not touch exposed wires
  • Do not reset tripped breakers repeatedly
  • Do not attempt DIY repairs

Call 911 first for active fire/heavy smoke, injury/shock needing medical attention, or downed power lines.
