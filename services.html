Panel Upgrades & Service Upgrades

If lights flicker, breakers trip, or you’re adding major loads, your electrical panel may be the bottleneck. We replace outdated panels and plan capacity so your home is ready for today—and for what you add next.

  • Electrical panel replacement (safe, organized, clearly labeled)
  • Service upgrades when capacity is undersized

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 charging at home should be fast, safe, and straightforward. We confirm capacity, size the circuit correctly, handle permits, and install a clean charger setup that won’t create nuisance trips later.
