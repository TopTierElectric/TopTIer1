Safe, code-compliant EV charger installation for homes and businesses across West Michigan.

Home charging should feel simple: plug in, wake up charged. The important part is making sure the circuit, breaker, and electrical panel capacity are planned correctly—because EV charging is a high, continuous load.

Typical range: $495–$895

What We Install

  • Residential Level 2 EV chargers (the most common and practical home option)
  • Commercial charging solutions where appropriate (including higher-power systems)
  • Dedicated circuits, clean mounting, and tidy finish work

What You Can Expect

  • Capacity check of your electrical panel and service
  • Correct circuit sizing and protection
  • Permit/inspection coordination when required
  • A clean installation that won’t create nuisance trips or unpredictable electrical behavior

Plug-In vs Hardwired (Quick Guidance)

  • Plug-in chargers can be convenient if you move often, but they require the right receptacle, protection, and a clean install.
  • Hardwired chargers typically look cleaner and are often the simplest long-term solution.

We’ll walk you through both and recommend what fits your layout and driving habits.

How We Work

  1. Walkthrough — Verify panel capacity and best charger location.
  2. Plan — Define the circuit, routing, and scope (no surprises).
  3. Execute — Install the dedicated circuit and mount the EVSE cleanly.
  4. Verify — Confirm operation, labeling, and safe settings.

FAQ

Do I need a panel upgrade to add a charger?
Not always. We check capacity first and recommend the simplest code-compliant path.

Do permits matter?
Most municipalities require them. We coordinate the process when needed.
