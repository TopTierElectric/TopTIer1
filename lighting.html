Lighting That Fits the Way You Use the Space

Great lighting balances visibility, comfort, and control. We help homeowners and small businesses across West Michigan choose and install fixtures that work for the space—not just what looks good in the box.

Indoor Lighting

We install recessed cans, pendants, under-cabinet lighting, and fixture replacements in kitchens, bathrooms, living rooms, and workspaces. We also help with new circuits for high-output fixtures when existing wiring isn’t enough.

Outdoor & Security Lighting

Exterior lighting adds safety and usability around entryways, driveways, garages, patios, and walkways. We install weather-rated fixtures, security lights, and motion controls with proper routing and protection.

Controls, Dimmers, and Smart Options

Dimmers, occupancy sensors, and timers make lighting more flexible and efficient. If you’re interested in smart controls, we can wire the system so it’s ready for compatible switches and hubs.

Our Installation Process

  1. Plan the layout: Identify fixture locations, switch points, and circuit needs.
  2. Confirm compatibility: Match fixture requirements to wiring and breaker capacity.
  3. Install and secure: Mount fixtures, run wiring, and tidy the finish work.
  4. Test and review: Confirm operation and walk through controls with you.

Lighting FAQs

Can you install customer-supplied fixtures?

Yes, as long as the fixture is compatible with your wiring and rated for the location.

Do I need new wiring for recessed lights?

Sometimes. We review access, existing wiring, and fixture requirements before confirming.

Can you add dimmers to existing switches?

Yes, if the fixtures and wiring support dimming. We confirm compatibility first.

Are outdoor fixtures different from indoor fixtures?

Outdoor fixtures must be weather-rated and installed with proper protection.

What if a fixture needs a dedicated circuit?

We can add a new circuit when the load requires it or when the panel is full.

Can you help with lighting layout ideas?

Yes. We help you choose fixture placement based on task areas and traffic flow.
