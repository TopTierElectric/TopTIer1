Recent Work

Pricing Factors

  • Fixture count and ceiling access
  • Switching/dimming complexity
  • Need for new circuits or dedicated lighting loads
  • Fixture type and mounting height

Permits & Inspection Readiness

If new circuits are required, we coordinate permits and ensure inspections pass cleanly.

Common Projects We Handle

  • Recessed and decorative fixture installation
  • Kitchen task lighting (including under-cabinet)
  • Exterior lighting for safety and curb appeal
  • LED upgrades and retrofits
  • Dimmers and smart controls that work the way they should

What You Can Expect

  • A walkthrough focused on how you use the space
  • Layout guidance (spacing, switch locations, and practical light levels)
  • Compatibility planning (fixtures + dimmers + LEDs to reduce flicker)
  • Clean installation: straight trims, tidy mounting, professional finish

How We Work

  1. Walkthrough — Identify goals: task, ambient, and accent needs.
  2. Plan — Layout + switching plan + fixture approach.
  3. Execute — Clean wiring and careful trim work.
  4. Verify — Test dimming, switching logic, and final appearance.

Why This Matters

Good lighting improves comfort, reduces eyestrain, and helps finishes look their best. Done cleanly and code-compliantly, it also reduces future troubleshooting and callbacks.
