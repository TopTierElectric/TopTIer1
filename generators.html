Generator Installation FAQs

How do I choose the right generator size?

We calculate load priorities and recommend a size based on what you want powered during an outage.

Do I need a transfer switch?

Yes. Transfer equipment is required for safe, code-compliant backup power operation.

Can you install whole-home backup?

We can scope whole-home or essential-circuit coverage depending on goals and budget.

Do you handle permits?

Yes. We coordinate permit and inspection steps required for the electrical portion.

Will the generator run automatically?

With the right setup, standby systems can start automatically when utility power drops.

Can I keep my current panel?

Often yes, but we verify panel condition and capacity during the walkthrough.

How long does installation take?

Timeline depends on scope, permitting, and equipment lead time. We provide a clear schedule before work begins.

Do you service existing transfer setups?

We can inspect and troubleshoot existing transfer equipment and associated circuits.

What fuel types do you support?

Electrical scope supports generator systems designed around common residential fuel options.

Do you provide homeowner walkthroughs?

Yes. We explain operation basics and labeling so you understand the system handoff.
