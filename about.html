Our Core Services

If you want the full scope details, each service below has a dedicated page on our site.

Panel Upgrades & Service Upgrades

When the panel is outdated, out of space, or undersized for modern loads, a panel/service upgrade is often the foundation for safe expansion. We focus on permit-ready planning, tidy installs, clear labeling, and inspection-ready execution.

Learn more: /panel-upgrades

EV Charger Installation (Level 2)

Home charging should feel simple — plug in and wake up charged. The important part is making sure the circuit sizing, breaker protection, and panel capacity are planned correctly because EV charging is a high continuous load.

Learn more: /ev-chargers

Lighting & Fixtures

Lighting should look intentional and work the way it should. We plan placement, switching, dimming, and compatibility so the result is clean, functional, and reliable — inside and out.

Learn more: /lighting

Electrical Repairs & Troubleshooting

Dead outlets, nuisance trips, partial power loss, flickering lights, warm devices — we troubleshoot safely, explain what we find, and outline clear fix options before work begins.

Learn more: /electrical-repairs

Generator Sales & Installation

Backup power should feel calm and predictable. We help you plan what you actually need to run during an outage, then install transfer equipment cleanly and safely with inspection-ready documentation where required.

Learn more: /generators

Our Process

Diagnose → Explain → Scope → Execute & Verify

  1. Listen & Diagnose — We start by understanding symptoms and checking the system for a safe, accurate diagnosis.
  2. Plan the Fix — You get clear options, scope boundaries, and a plan for routing, materials, and safety.
  3. Complete & Walk Through — We complete the work, test circuits, and walk you through what changed and how to use it.
