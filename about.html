About Top Tier Electrical

Top Tier Electrical is a locally owned and operated business serving West Michigan. Founded by a team of experienced electricians who are passionate about providing safe, reliable and innovative electrical solutions, we have built our reputation on integrity, quality workmanship and excellent customer service.


Our Mission

To empower our community by delivering high‑quality electrical services that improve comfort, efficiency and safety in homes and businesses. We strive to be the contractor of choice by treating every customer like family, educating them about their electrical systems and offering solutions that fit their needs and budget.


Our Values

  • Safety: We follow industry best practices and codes to protect you and your property.
  • Honesty: We provide upfront pricing and clear communication so there are no surprises.
  • Quality: We use top‑grade materials and take pride in our craftsmanship.
  • Innovation: We stay up to date with the latest technology, from smart home solutions to renewable energy.
  • Community: We support local events and charities and hire from within West Michigan.

Meet the Team

Our team is composed of licensed journeymen and master electricians, apprentices and project managers who bring decades of experience to each project. We invest in ongoing training to ensure our knowledge stays current with codes, safety standards and emerging technologies.
