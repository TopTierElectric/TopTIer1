Where We Work

Our team of licensed electricians travels throughout West Michigan to provide prompt, professional service. Here are some of the communities we serve:

  • Holland – From downtown to the lakeshore, we’re your hometown electricians.
  • Grand Rapids – Residential and commercial services throughout the metro area.
  • Byron Center – Fast, reliable service for growing neighborhoods.
  • Zeeland – Trusted by businesses and homeowners alike.
  • Saugatuck & Douglas – Resort and waterfront properties welcome.
  • Hudsonville & Jamestown – Farms, shops and homes in these rural communities.
