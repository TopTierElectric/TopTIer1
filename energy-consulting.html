Who Energy Consulting Is For

Energy consulting helps you plan electrical changes before you commit to equipment or construction. It’s especially useful when you’re preparing for EV charging, remodeling a kitchen or basement, adding a workshop, or considering a standby generator.

What You Get

  • Assessment of current panel capacity and circuit layout.
  • Prioritized recommendations for upgrades or corrections.
  • Clear upgrade paths so future additions are straightforward.
  • Coordination guidance for contractors or remodel timelines.

Our Process

  1. Discovery: We review your goals, equipment plans, and usage needs.
  2. Assessment: We evaluate the panel, circuits, and available capacity.
  3. Planning: We outline the best path forward with phased options.
  4. Next steps: We answer questions and provide scope-based estimates.

Energy Consulting FAQs

Who is energy consulting for?

It’s ideal for homeowners or small businesses planning EV chargers, remodels, workshops, or generator installs.

What do I receive after a consultation?

You receive an assessment summary, prioritized recommendations, and clear upgrade paths.

Can you review my panel capacity?

Yes. We evaluate capacity and explain whether a panel upgrade is needed.

Will you coordinate with other trades?

We can provide electrical guidance that aligns with your contractor’s schedule and needs.

Do you provide written recommendations?

We summarize findings and outline next steps so you can plan confidently.

Can you plan for future loads?

Yes. We help you stage upgrades so future additions are straightforward.

Is this only for large projects?

No. We also help with smaller plans like adding circuits or improving lighting controls.

Can you price the work after consulting?

We can provide scope-based estimates once the plan is defined.
